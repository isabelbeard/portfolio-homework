Texas-born, London-based graphic designer with a focus on influential and pertinent data visualizations. Isabel is an innovative and creative designer with a sophisticated eye for detail and a desire to solve problems through any means possible (when not on her Mac sifting through pivot tables in Excel or mocking up complex visualizations manually in Illustrator, she can be found sprawled out on her living room floor assembling a charity-shop puzzle with a dubious amount of pieces.) Self-described as a ‘non-coding visual artist’, she is currently dabbling in the world of code in order to better understand the tools at her disposal. She has a proven ability to produce high quality work under pressure for both short- and long-term deadlines. She has experience leading a team but can also adapt to working within a team environment.
